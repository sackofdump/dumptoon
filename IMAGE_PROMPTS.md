# Dumptoon TCG — Image Prompts

All 32 character prompts, fully assembled. Format per card:

> `{character description}, {master style}, {rarity modifier}`

**Master style (applied to every prompt):**
old-school cartoon style, thick black outlines, soft shading, vibrant but slightly muted colors, cartoon network style, simple readable shapes, highly expressive character, clean composition, character fills most of the frame, no empty space, soft lighting, subtle textured background, polished game art, centered subject

**Rarity modifiers:**
- COMMON — simple design, minimal effects, soft flat background, low visual complexity
- RARE — blue glow accents, more motion, light particle effects, slightly more detailed background
- EPIC — purple energy effects, dynamic lighting, strong motion, magical or electric aura, high detail
- LEGENDARY — gold/orange glow, dramatic lighting, cinematic pose, powerful aura, high contrast, epic scale, intense effects

**Suggested image-gen settings:**
- Aspect ratio: `3:4` (card portrait)
- Negative prompt: `text, watermark, logo, signature, low quality, blurry, deformed, extra limbs, photorealistic, 3d render`
- For Midjourney append: `--ar 3:4 --style raw --stylize 250`
- For SD/SDXL: 768x1024, CFG 7, 30 steps

Save outputs as `Characters/{Rarity}/{Card Name}.png` to match the lookup the site uses.
